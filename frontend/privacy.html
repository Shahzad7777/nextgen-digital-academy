Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. This policy explains how NextGen Digital Academy handles student, visitor, and enrollment information.

1. Introduction

NextGen Digital Academy is an online education platform led by Shahzad Hassan, focused on digital marketing, online business, sales funnels, paid advertising, mentorship, and practical skill development for students, freelancers, and business owners, especially in the Pakistani market.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website, submit a form, comment on our content, contact us through WhatsApp, or enroll in any course or mentorship program.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect basic information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, course interest, payment confirmation details, comments, questions, and messages submitted through forms or direct contact.

We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, time spent on the website, and general analytics data to improve website performance and student experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to respond to inquiries, process enrollments, provide course access, manage student support, improve website content, approve or moderate comments, send important course updates, and improve our marketing and educational experience.

We do not sell your personal information. We only use it for legitimate education, support, communication, security, and service improvement purposes.

4. Payment Information

NextGen Digital Academy may support local Pakistani payment methods such as Bank Alfalah, Easypaisa, or other bank/mobile wallet transfers. We may request payment screenshots or transaction references for verification.

We do not ask you to submit sensitive banking passwords, OTPs, PINs, or full card details. Never share these sensitive details with anyone claiming to represent the academy.

5. Comments and Public Content

When you submit a comment, review, or question, it may be reviewed by the admin before appearing publicly. Approved comments may display your name and message. Your email address will not be publicly displayed unless you include it inside the comment text yourself.

6. LocalStorage and Browser Data

The website may use browser storage such as LocalStorage to remember course data, comments, admin-managed content, and user-interface preferences. This improves loading speed and supports a lightweight static hosting architecture. You can clear browser storage from your browser settings at any time.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party scripts in the future to understand traffic, improve content, and measure marketing performance.

8. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted service providers when necessary for hosting, analytics, communication, payment confirmation, email delivery, or technical support. We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, fraud prevention, platform protection, or user safety.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or loss. However, no website, browser storage, internet transmission, or digital platform can guarantee complete security.

10. Student Responsibilities

Students are responsible for providing accurate contact and payment verification details. Students should not share private login details, course access links, payment credentials, or academy material with unauthorized users.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is intended for learners who can responsibly participate in online education. If a minor uses the platform, they should do so with permission and guidance from a parent or guardian.

12. Your Rights

You may contact us to request correction, removal, or review of information you have submitted, subject to reasonable verification and operational requirements.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows, new features are added, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be available on this page.

14. Contact

For privacy-related questions, enrollment support, or data requests, contact NextGen Digital Academy through the official website or official WhatsApp contact listed on the platform.
